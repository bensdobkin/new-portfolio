RCC X Shopify — NFT.NYC

New York City

Designed a bespoke members-only vending machine and on-screen UI powered by Shopify for RCC's activation at NFT.NYC.

Role

Lead Designer

Client

Shopify

Agency

Deeplocal

Shipped

June 2022

RCC (Random Character Collective) and Shopify came to Deeplocal to create an immersive and interactive experience at NFT.NYC, offering attendees a unique blend of entertainment, exclusive merchandise, and engaging token holder interactions. The activation combined a Merch Shop, Hall of Fame, and Club Lounge to elevate the event experience.

I designed all of the digital screens and corresponding loading animations for a token-gated vending machine in a skate-themed merch shop powered by Shopify. The concept integrated blockchain technology — specifically NFT ownership — with a traditional vending machine, creating an exciting and interactive way for token holders to receive merch from the machine.

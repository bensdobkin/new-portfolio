Microsoft Windows 11 Launch

Sydney · London · New York

Crafted an interactive and educational product experience inside 3 Microsoft flagship stores in Sydney, London, and New York City to mark the launch of Windows 11.

Role

Lead Designer

Client

Microsoft

Agency

Deeplocal

Shipped

Oct 2021

Microsoft came to Deeplocal to introduce Windows 11 to in-store visitors in a way that went beyond a spec sheet. Across flagship stores in Sydney, London, and New York, customers could walk up to a set of interactive displays and feel out the new operating system through the features that defined it.

I worked on the in-store product experience, helping shape the interactive moments that taught features like Snap Assist and Microsoft Teams integration through hands-on play rather than passive demos.

I also prepared the print files for the other side of the lenticular wall — the Windows 11 bloom — so the graphic landed cleanly across the physical install.

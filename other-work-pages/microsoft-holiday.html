Microsoft Holiday Window Display

Sydney · London · New York

Created animations for the holiday window display across 3 Microsoft flagship stores in Sydney, London, and New York City.

Role

Lead Animator

Client

Microsoft

Agency

Deeplocal

Shipped

Dec 2021

Microsoft came to Deeplocal to bring its holiday campaign to life across the windows of its flagship stores in Sydney, London, and New York. The result was a series of animated character moments that turned each storefront into a public holiday display passersby could pause and enjoy.

I designed and animated holiday window pieces tailored to each location, working alongside the broader Deeplocal team to keep the characters, motion, and pacing consistent across all three windows.

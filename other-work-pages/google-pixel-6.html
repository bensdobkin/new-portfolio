Google Pixel 6 Launch

New York City

I led UX and creative-directed nine in-store animations for Pixel 6's launch inside Google Store NYC — turning the phone's most advanced camera features into a walk-through gallery that opened on launch day.

Role

Creative Director
Lead Designer

Client

Google

Agency

Deeplocal

Shipped

October 2021

Google came to Deeplocal to craft an interactive experience for guest to experience the power of Pixel 6's camera. The experience is set in the flagship Google Store in NYC, specifically inside a Sandbox room: rooms inside the Google Store that bring the magic of their hardware to life.

I creative directed several animators creating nine onscreen animations that allowed guests to experience Pixel 6's newest camera features. The goal of the animations was to demonstrate the power of Pixel's camera while fostering a gallery feel to showcase the high quality photo output. I also assisted in final animation updates on-site where needed.
